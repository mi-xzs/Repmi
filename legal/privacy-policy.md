## 2. What data we collect

We have organised this by feature, so you can see what each part of the app collects. Where a field is voluntary, we mark it **(optional)**.

### 2.1 Account
- **Email address** — used to sign you in and send transactional emails (password reset, security notices).
- **Password** — set by you. We never see your password in clear text; it is hashed and stored by our authentication provider (Supabase).
- **Authentication session token (JWT)** — generated by Supabase and stored on your device so you stay signed in.
- **User ID (UUID)** — generated automatically. Used to link your account to your data.

### 2.2 Profile
- **Username** — chosen by you. Public on your profile and on leaderboards.
- **Age** (optional) — used to personalise the experience and shown on your profile if you make it public.
- **Body weight** (optional) — used to calculate workout volume in your chosen unit and shown on your profile if you make it public.
- **Height** (optional) — used to personalise the experience and shown on your profile if you make it public.
- **Training goal** (optional) — for example, strength, hypertrophy, endurance, weight loss, general fitness.
- **Weekly workout target** (optional).
- **Profile photo and cover image** (optional). **Important:** uploaded images are stored in publicly accessible buckets, which means anyone with the file URL can view them. The URLs are not advertised outside the app, but they are not access-controlled.

We treat **age, body weight, and height** as health-related data and process them on the basis of your explicit consent (see Section 4).

### 2.3 Workout logging
- **Workout templates** you create — names, exercises, sets, target reps, target weights, phases.
- **Workout sessions** you record — date, duration, exercises performed, sets with weight (kg), reps, time (minutes, seconds), labels.
- **Effort ratings (RPE)** (optional) — recorded per workout.
- **Water intake count** (optional) — stored only on your device.

### 2.4 Social features
- **Follow relationships** — who you follow and who follows you.
- **Cached statistics** for your public profile and leaderboard standing — total XP, weekly XP, total sessions, total volume lifted, current and longest streak, total reps, total sets, personal-record count. These are derived from your workout data and refreshed when you log a session.
- **Shared workout links** — when you share a workout, we generate a public link that anyone who receives it can open in the app. The link reveals the workout's content and the username of the person who shared it.

### 2.5 Settings & preferences
- Unit preferences (kg/lb, cm/ft).
- Privacy controls (public profile on/off, open follows on/off, water tracker on/off).
- Equipped cosmetic items (titles, themes) and coin balance.

### 2.6 Device & technical
- **Operating system and app version** — sent automatically when the app talks to our backend, so we can support older versions and diagnose issues.
- **IP address** — collected by our backend (Supabase) at the network level for security and abuse-prevention purposes. We do not store IP addresses tied to your account.
- **Notifications permission** — local notifications only (e.g., rest-timer reminders). We do **not** request a push-notification token and we do **not** send notifications from our servers.
- **Media-library permission** — requested only when you choose to upload a profile photo or cover image.

### 2.7 What we do NOT collect
- We do **not** use analytics, attribution, or A/B-testing SDKs.
- We do **not** collect precise or coarse location.
- We do **not** access your camera, microphone, contacts, calendar, or motion sensors.
- We do **not** read your other files or photos beyond the single image you choose to upload.
- We do **not** track you across other companies' apps or websites. As a result, the iOS App Tracking Transparency prompt does not appear.

## 9. How we protect your data

- **Encryption in transit** — all communication between the app and our backend is over TLS (HTTPS).
- **Encryption at rest** — data stored in Supabase is encrypted at rest at the infrastructure level.
- **Access controls** — only the developer needs administrative access to the backend, and that access is protected by strong passwords and multi-factor authentication.
- **Minimisation** — we do not collect data we do not need, and we do not collect any analytics or telemetry.
- **Public buckets caveat** — profile photos and cover images are stored in publicly readable storage buckets. The URLs are not advertised, but they are not access-controlled. Do not upload images you would not want a stranger with the URL to see.
- **No system is perfectly secure.** We cannot guarantee absolute security and we encourage you to use a strong, unique password and to enable any device-level security available to you.

## 14. Breach notification

If a personal-data breach is likely to result in a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will notify the competent supervisory authority within **72 hours** of becoming aware, in line with Article 33 GDPR. Where the breach is likely to result in a **high risk**, we will also notify affected users without undue delay, in line with Article 34. For US users whose health-related information is affected, we will also follow the **60-day** timeline required by the FTC Health Breach Notification Rule (see Section 7.4).

## 15. Automated decision-making

We do not make decisions about you based solely on automated processing that produce legal or similarly significant effects.

The leaderboard ranks users by derived statistics, and the achievements feature awards titles based on workout activity. These are transparency-friendly rankings, not decisions about you in the sense of Article 22.
