## 2. What "consumer health data" means here

"Consumer health data" includes any personal information that identifies your past, present, or future physical or mental health status. Under MHMDA, this expressly includes **measurements of bodily functions, exercise, and fitness**.

For Repmi, the following data is consumer health data:

- Workout sessions and the exercises, sets, weights, reps, durations, and effort ratings (RPE) you record.
- Workout templates you create.
- Body weight, height, and age that you choose to enter into your profile.
- Derived statistics about your training (total volume, streaks, personal-record counts).
- Identifiers (user ID, email, username) when associated with any of the above.

Profile photos, follow relationships, and pure technical data (operating system, app version) on their own are **not** consumer health data.

## 3. What we collect and where we get it

We collect consumer health data **directly from you** when you use the app — when you log a workout, edit your profile, or rate effort.

We also **derive** consumer health data from what you log (for example, total volume lifted in a week is calculated from your sets and reps).

We do **not** purchase, license, or otherwise obtain consumer health data from third parties.

## 4. Why we process it and what we do with it

We use your consumer health data only to:

- store and display your workouts, templates, and training history within the app;
- compute and display your statistics, streaks, achievements, and leaderboard standing;
- show your profile (including stats you choose to make public) to other users;
- operate, secure, and improve the Service;
- comply with our legal obligations.

We do **not** use consumer health data for advertising, profiling, or sale.

## 5. Our consent practices

- **Collection necessary to provide the Service** (logging a workout you typed in, displaying it to you) is performed without separate opt-in consent, because it is necessary to deliver what you asked for.
- **Optional fields** — age, body weight, height, and effort ratings — are collected only after you provide them; entering them is your opt-in.
- **Any future use of consumer health data beyond what is necessary to provide the Service** (for example, optional analytics) will be introduced only with your separate, distinct, opt-in consent. We will ask you in a prompt that is not bundled with any other agreement, and you will be able to withdraw the consent at any time in Settings.

You may **withdraw consent** for any optional consumer health data processing at any time by:

## 6. Sharing of consumer health data

We share your consumer health data only with **service providers** (sometimes called "processors") that act strictly on our instructions to operate the Service. Specifically:

- **Supabase Inc.** (United States) — our authentication, database, and file-storage provider.
- **Apple Inc.** and **Google LLC** to the extent they receive data through app distribution and the operating-system services you use.

These providers are contractually bound to process your data only on our instructions and to maintain confidentiality.

We do **not**:

- sell consumer health data;
- share consumer health data for advertising or cross-context behavioural advertising;
- share consumer health data with affiliates outside the limited category of service providers listed above (we have none);
- use consumer health data to identify, locate, or contact you for any purpose other than operating the Service.

## 7. Sale of consumer health data — we do not sell

We do **not** sell your consumer health data, and we will not sell it without your prior, written, signed authorisation that meets the requirements of RCW 19.373.060 (Washington) or NRS 603A.430 (Nevada). If our practices ever change, we will obtain your authorisation first.

## 8. Your rights

If you are a Washington or Nevada resident, you have the right to:

- **Confirm** whether we are processing your consumer health data and **access** the consumer health data we hold about you;
- **Receive a list** of all third parties and affiliates with whom we have shared or sold your consumer health data (see Section 6 for our current practices);
- **Withdraw consent** for any consumer health data we collect or share based on consent;
- **Request deletion** of your consumer health data. On a verified deletion request, we will delete your consumer health data from our active systems and instruct our processors to do the same.

**How to make a request.** Email **<!-- TODO: REPLACE_BEFORE_PUBLISH: PRIVACY CONTACT EMAIL -->REPLACE_BEFORE_PUBLISH** with the email address on your Repmi account and a description of the request. We may need to verify your identity in a proportionate way.

**How long we take.** We respond within **45 days** of receiving a verifiable request. We may extend this once by a further 45 days where reasonably necessary, taking into account the complexity and number of requests. We will tell you about any extension within the first 45-day window.

**Appeals.** If we refuse to act on your request, you can appeal by replying to our response within 60 days. We will respond to your appeal within 45 days. If your appeal is denied, you can contact the **Washington Attorney General** (https://www.atg.wa.gov/file-complaint) or the **Nevada Attorney General** (https://ag.nv.gov/) to file a complaint.

## 9. Geofencing prohibition

Repmi does **not** use geofences. We do not collect precise location at all. We do not, and will not, use a geofence around any healthcare facility to identify or track consumers seeking healthcare, to collect consumer health data, or to send advertising or notifications related to consumer health data.

## 10. Security and retention

Consumer health data is stored on the same secure infrastructure described in our main [Privacy Policy](privacy-policy.md), Section 9. We retain consumer health data for the periods described in Section 10 of the main Privacy Policy. When you delete your account, your consumer health data is deleted within 30 days from active systems and within 90 days from backups, and processors are instructed to do the same.

## 11. Changes to this policy

If we make a material change to this Consumer Health Data Privacy Policy, we will notify you at least **30 days** before the change takes effect, by email and in the app.
