# Delete your Repmi account

This page describes how to delete your Repmi account and the data we hold about you. It satisfies Google Play's account-deletion web-URL requirement and is also linked from the app's privacy policy.

**You do not need the Repmi app installed to use this page.**

---

## Option 1 — Delete from inside the app (fastest)

1. Open Repmi.
2. Tap **Profile** → **Settings**.
3. Scroll to **Account**.
4. Tap **Delete account**.
5. Confirm the prompt.

Your account is queued for deletion immediately, and you are signed out.

## What gets deleted

- Your account (email, password hash, user ID).
- Your profile (username, age, body weight, height, training goal, weekly target, avatar, cover image).
- All your workout templates, workout sessions, and effort ratings (RPE).
- Your follows (the relationships in which you are the follower or the followed).
- Your cached statistics (XP, streaks, totals, personal-record counts).
- Your shared workout links and the workouts behind them.
- Your settings and preferences.
- Your coin balance and any cosmetic items you have unlocked.
- Server-side logs containing your data, within 90 days.

Once deletion is complete, the data above cannot be recovered.

## What we keep, and for how long

We retain a minimal amount of information after deletion when we are legally required to or to defend against legal claims:

- **Email correspondence** with us (e.g., your deletion request itself) — up to 24 months from closure.
- **Records required by law** — for the period the law requires.
- **Backups** — purged within 90 days of deletion.
- **Aggregated, anonymised information** that no longer identifies you (for example, total number of users in a country) — indefinitely. This information cannot be linked back to you.

## What happens to your public content

Public content you have shared with other users — for example, workouts you sent to friends through a share link — is removed from our servers when you delete your account. Any copy a recipient saved locally before deletion is outside our control.

## Withdrawing a deletion request

You can withdraw a deletion request within 7 days of submitting it by replying to our confirmation email. After 7 days, the deletion proceeds and cannot be reversed.
